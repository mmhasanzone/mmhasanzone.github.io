Right Form of Verb Rules & Practice

1. 3rd Person Singular subject-এর সাথে verb-এর s/es যোগ হয়। (He plays football.)
2. Past simple tense-এ verb-এর past form ব্যবহৃত হয়। (They went home.)
3. Future simple tense-এ will/shall + base form হয়। (She will dance tomorrow.)
4. Auxiliary verb (is/am/are/was/were) + verb-এর ing form হয়। (She is singing now.)
5. Have verb (has/have/had) + past participle হয়। (They have finished the work.)
6. Modal verb (can, may, must, should) + base form হয়। (You should study more.)
7. Preposition-এর পর Gerund (verb + ing) হয়। (He is good at singing.)
8. Causative Verbs (Make, Let, Have, Get) অনুযায়ী verb-এর রূপ পরিবর্তিত হয়।
9. Need এবং Dare modal verb হলে পরবর্তী verb-এর base form হয়।
10. Would Rather / Had Better + Base Form হয়। (I would rather stay home.)
11. It’s High Time + Past Verb হয়। (It’s high time you started studying.)
12. Wish এবং If Only-এর পর Past Tense হয়। (I wish I were a bird.)
13. Would You Mind + Verb + ing হয়। (Would you mind helping me?)
14. As If / As Though + Past Tense হয়। (He talks as if he knew everything.)
15. Passive Voice-এ Modal Verb + be + Past Participle হয়।
16. Neither...nor / Either...or অনুযায়ী verb ব্যবহৃত হয়।
17. Every, Each, Either, Neither, Someone, Nobody-এর পর Singular Verb হয়।
18. Since / For-এর পর Present Perfect বা Present Perfect Continuous Tense হয়।
19. Would That + Subject + Could/Would + Base Form হয়।
20. Let’s, Let Us-এর পর Base Form হয়।
21. Hardly/Scarcely... when এবং No sooner... than-এর পর Past Perfect হয়।
22. Lest-এর পর Should + Base Form হয়।
23. Despite/In spite of-এর পর Gerund হয়।
24. Superlative Degree-এর পর Present Perfect Tense হয়।
25. Could / Might / Should / Would Have + Past Participle হয়।
26. It is time + Past Tense হয়।
27. Too... to + Base Form হয়।
28. Be + Used to + Gerund হয়।
29. Would You Like + To + Base Form হয়।
30. As Well As / Along With / Together With / With / Accompanied By-এর পর Singular Verb হয়।
31. One of + Plural Noun + Singular Verb হয়।
32. More Than One + Singular Noun + Singular Verb হয়।
33. There is / There are অনুযায়ী verb ব্যবহৃত হয়।
34. As Soon As-এর পর Present Simple হলে, পরবর্তী অংশ Future Tense হয়।
35. Prefer / Preferable-এর সঠিক ব্যবহার।
36. Found / Catch / See / Hear / Watch + Object + Verb + ing হয়।
37. Advice / Suggest-এর সঠিক ব্যবহার।
38. Hardly / Scarcely... When এবং No Sooner... Than-এর পর Past Perfect হয়।
39. On + Verb + ing (On seeing, On hearing) হয়।
40. Possessive Pronoun-এর পর Gerund হয়।
41. The number of + Singular Verb হয়।
42. A number of + Plural Verb হয়।
43. Many a + Singular Noun + Singular Verb হয়।
44. If-Clause Type 1: Present Simple + Future Simple হয়।
45. If-Clause Type 2: Past Simple + Would/Could + Base Form হয়।
46. If-Clause Type 3: Past Perfect + Would Have + Past Participle হয়।
47. While + Past Continuous হলে, অন্য অংশটি Past Simple হয়।
48. Before-এর পর Past Perfect হলে, প্রধান বাক্যে Past Simple হয়।
49. Until / Till-এর পর Negative Meaning থাকলে Affirmative Verb হয়।
50. Would Prefer + To + Base Form হয়।
